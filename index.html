S. T. Williams

writer of all sorts

A very nice young man from Naarm, S. T. Williams (he/him) is currently studying a Bachelor of Arts at RMIT University. He lives on and writes from Wurundjeri Country. S. T.'s piece, Dust Becomes the Sun, appears in the 2025 edition of the Bowen Street Press Review, sunder.

With a desire to write stories that make people cry bitter tears, laugh with seismic gusto, and tremble with volcanic envy (makes them all sick), there’s no one who can stop him from seizing the literary power he so craves.
